ABOUT ME

Hey! I see you found my site. I'm Wendy, a Graphic Designer who works across digital platforms, social media and print. I have been in the creative industry for over 10 years, working for a variety of companies gaining valuable skills and insight into the industry. Most recently I have been in a multi-disciplined role within QA's in-house Creative Team.

My objective is to always put the user first, primarily through a strong sense of structure. Identifying their needs, mapping their journey and simplifying communication are key stages in the way that I design.

Design is a big part of my life, I want to be part of a team that solves problems, innovates and challenges convention, so don't be shy, say hello!
